Doctor–Patient Conversation

Doctor: Good morning. Please have a seat. How are you feeling today?
Patient: Good morning, Doctor. I haven't been feeling well for the past two weeks.
Doctor: Can you describe your main symptoms?
Patient: I have persistent headaches and occasional dizziness.
Doctor: Your BP is 158/98 — that is Stage 2 Hypertension.
Patient: That sounds serious, Doctor.
Doctor: It requires attention but is manageable with medication and lifestyle changes.
Patient: What should I do next?
Doctor: Start medication, reduce salt intake, exercise daily, and monitor your BP.
